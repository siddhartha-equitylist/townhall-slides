How we work

We moved to a sprint culture

Before June we had a roadmap, but execution was reactive. Priorities shifted without structure, and it was hard to know what was actually in flight. In June we moved to a structured sprint framework, so that:

Structured tasks for dev

Work reaches engineering with clearer scope, ownership, and priority before it starts.

Clear goal-based sprints

Each sprint has a focused goal, so the team can plan around outcomes instead of loose lists.

Reduced ad hocs

Incoming asks get weighed against sprint commitments instead of interrupting the flow.
